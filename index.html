Hello, welcome to my webpage! My name is Eric Dammann and I am a student at Montclair State University majoring in Marine Biology and Coastal Sciences. I am a member of the MSU Coastal Dynamics Lab working under my advisor Dr. Jorge Lorenzo-Trueba. I am interested in nearly every physical, biological or chemical process that takes place within coastal environments.

Make sure to check out my Research and Publications pages which detail the motivation of my research and my publicaiton history respectively. The About Me page gives you a little bit of information about me as a person outside of research and academia. The Philosophy page outlines my philosophical motivation for the research I do and the Quotes page includes a collection of my favorite quotes. If you are interested in my work and want to connect with me, all my information can be found on the Contact page or on my CV which can be found here.
